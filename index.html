Equilibrium: Why are Glaciers Melting?

The glaciers are melting because human activity is causing temperatures and pollution to rise, eating away at our ozone layer and allowing in more raw light and heat from the sun.

What Can We Do To Help?

  • 1. Always recycle
  • 2. Carpool when possible
  • 3. Stay away from single-use plastics
  • 4. Only purchase from humanely sourced brands
  • 5. Plant & Garden to cultivate oxygen production
